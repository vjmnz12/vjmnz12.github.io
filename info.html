5 Facts About the Dominican Republic

  • Positioned in the heart of the Caribbean, the Dominican Republic graces the island of Hispaniola with its presence.
  • The vibrant heartbeat of the Dominican Republic resonates from Santo Domingo, its bustling capital and largest city.
  • Spanish stands as the primary language spoken throughout the Dominican Republic, reflecting its rich linguistic heritage.
  • The Dominican Republic's economic vitality stems from its flourishing tourism industry, robust agricultural sector, and diverse service offerings.
  • Embark on a sensory journey through the Dominican Republic's cultural tapestry, woven with the rhythms of music, the grace of dance, and the flavors of its cuisine.
